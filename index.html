The valuable contributions of photography to our lives

Practicing photography has many more benefits than you might think. When you start taking pictures, you will not only begin to see things from a different perspective, but you will also develop a series of skills and abilities that will help you not only be a better photographer, but also a better person. Photography is an art that will require concentration, discipline and a lot of passion. You will see how, by practicing it, your vision of the world will expand a little more, you will be able to connect more with yourself and with the people around you. So, if you are going to start taking pictures, go ahead and grab your camera, which will not only be your tool, but also your companion on the journey through this fun and enriching path that is photography.

This helps with
self-knowledge

As you practice photography, you will realize that you need to learn more about yourself, as you need to be sure of what you want to photograph in the first place. This requires a process of self-knowledge, because you need to explore what you like to do, what you want to express and how you are going to achieve it. Photography is a discipline that will require you to learn, not only about photographic technique, but about yourself, as you progress. With it, we express our feelings in a creative way, allowing us to express ourselves about our emotions, personal experiences, what we like and even what we don't like, everything that excites us, for all this and much more, photography has great value as a means of expression.

Develop creativity

Photography will help you develop your creativity. If you want to take different photos, you need to connect with your creative side, and when you start practicing it with more discipline, you start doing a bit of “brain gymnastics,” as Gastón Enria says in his podcast. You will see how your vision of the world will expand and you will see everything with different eyes.

She teaches us to have
different perspectives

Furthermore, photography, by awakening the curiosity to see things from different angles, teaches us to have different perspectives. Not only in terms of aesthetics, but in general, you will see that by practicing it, you will see things from a different point of view. Remember that no one sees the world the way you see it. Remember that you can transform ordinary places into epic places if you put your mind to it. Likewise, even if they say that a familiar place has already been photographed a thousand times, your vision is a matter of perspective, photograph it anyway.
